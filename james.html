DR. JAMES ADOMAKO

Floral Ecologist


Dr. James Adomako is a product of the University of Ghana where he obtained a BSc. (Hons) Botany with Zoology; and then MPhil and PhD in Ecology. He also holds a certificate in Diatom Taxonomy from the University College of London.
Dr. Adomako is a renowned ecologist with over 25 years of experience in consultancy and has been part of a plethora of ESIA studies on many national and international projects. He has consulted for many institutions including those in the Mining industries, Landfill Sites Development, Airport expansion works and for other institutions in the Water Sector, and Forestry Commission of Ghana. He was part of the team that worked on the West Africa Gas Pipeline from Nigeria to Ghana. His works also expand to cover the review of relevant policies, regulations and law. He is the Team Lead of the Flora and Revegetation team of ESL Monitoring Team of the Construction Phase activities of the ENI OCTP/ORF at Sanzule, monitoring impacts of construction phase activities on ecosystem provisioning and regulating services, identification and monitoring of the proliferation of Alien Invasive Species and revegetation of restored areas. Other works James has been involved as Terrestrial Ecologist include, Consultant for WWF-International on the Biodiversity Convention, National Expert on Mangroves for the GEF Gulf of Guinea Large Marine Ecosystem Project, Mapping of sudd vegetation on the Kpong head pond, Environmental Audit of small scale mining operations in Ghana, Studies on the Underlying Causes of Deforestation and Forest Degradation in Ghana, Ecological Baseline Studies for Korle Lagoon, Floral Survey of the Biodiversity Component of the NRMP, Forestry Commission, Ethnobotanical Survey in Inland Valley Sites, Inland Valleys Rice Development.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
