The Environmental Assessment and Management Unit, together with the Communication and Social Development Unit undertake most of the consulting assignments for the company. The Consultancy section of the company has the right expertises to meet the demands of our clients.


Most of the assignments undertaken by our team of experts are funded by development partners such as the World Bank, IFC, AfDB, USTDA, DANIDA, DFID, and NORDIC. Our experts are therefore very conversant with international standards and formats for Environmental Compliance Reports. With this track record, clients' choice to engage the services of NEMAS gives them the utmost assurance of security and excellent quality.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
