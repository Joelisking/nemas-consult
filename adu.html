MR. ADU NYARKO ANDORFUL

Social and Resettlement Expert


Mr. Adu-Nyarko is an astute Social Consultation Specialist with over 12 years of exposure in Community Development, Impact Assessment studies, Socio-Economic survey, Resettlement Action Plans, Formulation of Compensation Policies and Regulatory Framework, Evaluation and Monitoring of projects; and has been part of a panel reviewing ESIA reports. Regarding experience in the mining sector, Adu-Nyarko has worked on many projects for mining companies in Ghana and elsewhere. Projects in have included Anglogold Ashanti, where he was the team leader in assessing land use of Dokyiwa in relation to Tailings Storage Facility, and Golden Star Resources-Bogoso/Prestea Ltd., where he led a ten-member team to undertake a comprehensive socio-economic survey and assets inventory in the Environmental and Social Impact Assessment (ESIA) of the company's Mampon-Abronye Project in the Opon Manse Forest Reserve (OMFR). He was involved in the Anikoko Resettlement Action Plan for the same company as well. Adu-Nyarko has been part of projects for Perseus Mining Ghana Ltd, then known as Ashanti Gold Ltd; Chirano Gold Mines, and Geomatrix/Newmont Ghana Gold Ltd. He has also worked for The Chamber of Mines on an International Consultative Meeting project to assess Compensation Policies, Practices and Procedures in the Tanzanian Mining Industry in Dar-Es-Salaam, Buzwagi, Kabanga and Geita â€“ Tanzania. In a closely related development, he was worked with The Minerals Commission of Ghana in developing Corporate Social Responsibility Guidelines for Mining Companies in Mining Communities of Ghana, an assignment that brought him in association with all the major mining companies in Ghana. Other reputable institutions who have benefited from Mr Adu-Nyarko's expertise include, Tullow Oil Ghana, during the Designing and Implementation of a Communication Strategy for (Tullow-led) Jubilee Joint Venture Ghana; and during the preparation of the Scoping Report for the development of Tullow, Ghana Limited Tweneboa-Enyera-Ntomme (T.E.N.) Project towards the installation of the second FPSO in the Western Region. Additionally, he has worked for the Forestry Commission of Ghana, Golder and Associates, Watertech Ghana, Lonrho Ports, and Ministry of Agriculture. From 2007-2009, he was the EU/GOG Research Consultant on Environmental Impact Assessment/Strategic Environmental Assessment, National Environmental and Social Impact Assessment of Mining and Exploration Areas in the Ghana Mining Sector Support Programme.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
