PAUL ACKAH

Safety, Health, And Environment Specialist


Paul Ackah holds a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Meteorology and Climate Science and a Master of Science (MSc) in Energy and Sustainability Management from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST). He also possesses several professional certifications, including an Occupational Health and Safety (OSHA) certificate from OSHAcademy, USA; an ICAM Lead Investigator certificate from SafetyWise, Australia; a Health and Safety Officer Competency Certificate from the Inspectorate Division of the Minerals Commission, Ghana; and an ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health & Safety Management System Awareness Certificate.

With over nine years of experience, Paul is an expert in safety, health, and environmental (SHE) management across the mining, construction, and aviation sectors. He has led the development and implementation of HSE management systems, provided strategic health and safety advice, and designed risk mitigation strategies to enhance operational safety. His achievements include leading multiple teams to secure ISO certifications, including ISO 45001:2018, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 9001:2015, as part of African and Middle East (AME) Team implemented Weir Zero harm gap analysis program throughout the region; AngloGold Ashanti Obuasi Mine for a period of one and half years contributing to the successful completion of complex projects with zero Lost Time Injuries (LTI), established and implemented Safety, Health and Environment management system at Weir Mineral Senegal.

Paul has also established safety, health, and environmental management systems in diverse operational environments and facilitated regional safety programs focused on achieving zero harm. Beyond his technical expertise, he serves as an occupational health and safety course facilitator at the KNUST-Africa Institute of Sanitation and Waste Management (KAISWAM) His leadership and extensive knowledge in SHE management continue to support sustainable, safe, and efficient operations across industries.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
