MR. EBENEZER QUADZIE

Environmental Quality and Surveillance Analyst


Mr. Ebenezer Quadzie holds a BSc in Water and Sanitation from the University of Cape Coast, Ghana and an MSc. in Environmental Science (specializing in Air Quality Surveillance) from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology Kumasi, Ghana. He also has a certificate in Occupational Health and Safety Training from OSHAcademy.
Mr. Quadzie has over 7 years’ postgraduate experience in Environmental Media Quality Monitoring and Analysis, Equipment Maintenance and Calibration, Data Analysis and Interpretation, using various Environmental Quality Software. He has undertaken Environmental Baseline Monitoring Studies as an integral part of Environmental Impact Assessment in 9 regions out of the then 10 regions of Ghana, involving the mining/quarry, agricultural, water and energy sectors. For instance, he was the team lead for air, noise, and water quality assessment for the following projects: Cardinal-Namdini Gold Mining Project, Kingsperp-Aboaboso Gold Mining Project, VAMED Nederland BV-Government of Ghana Hospital Project, Village Corps Biomass Power Plant/Farming System Project, and Atlantic Lithium Baseline Studies, which were all internationally funded projects and guided by World Bank/IFC Standards. In addition to baseline surveys, Ebenezer has successfully undertaken Fenceline Air Quality and Pollution Surveillance for over thirty (30) functional industries, including Paint Manufacturing, Bulk Oil Storage, Oil & Gas, Cement production, Alcoholic and Non-Alcoholic Beverages, Fertilizer production and Canned Food industries, either as a Regulatory requirement by the Ghana EPA and/or as a Company’s requirement for tracking internal pollution or assessing efficiency of production equipment and machines. Years of practical experience and training both on the field and at the laboratory have shaped his professional life into a robust and analytically oriented person. Ebenezer is an Environmental Quality Analyst at the Environmental Quality and Surveillance Unit at NEMAS Consult.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
