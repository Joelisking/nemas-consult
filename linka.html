MS. LINKA DADZIE

Communication and Social Development Expert


Ms. Linka holds a B.A. in Psychology and Management from the University of Ghana, an MSc. in Communication and Marketing from the Franklin University, Ohio-USA and a Certificate in Advanced Management from SMC University Switzerland eCampus Ghana.
Linka is a perceptive Community Engagement and Social Consultation Specialist with distinct communication and writing skills, and has a working knowledge in French as well. In Ghana, Ms. Dadzie has been involved in Impact Assessment studies, Socio-economic surveys, as well as Environmental and Social Due Diligence Surveys. She has either led or been part of Social Safeguards teams for internationally funded projects such as the Cardinal-Namdini Gold Mining Project, VAMED Nederland BV - Government of Ghana Hospital Project, Atlantic Lithium Baseline Studies, which involved extensive community engagements and a comprehensive socio-economic survey of host communities within Project’s catchment areas. She has conducted extensive Community Engagements as part of the Upper-East (Ghana) Region Water Supply Project for 8 major communities; and the EU-funded Upper-West (Ghana) Feeder Roads Project, in 6 of the 8 Districts in the Region. She was part of a team that conducted social risk assessment surveys for the Ghana Dedicated Grant Mechanism Project, and handled subsequent Grievance Redress Training for project committees of beneficiary communities of the project. Linka led a 5-member team to undertake a socio-economic survey and assets inventory in Environmental and Social Due Diligence Assessment (ESDDA) for 36 sites and surrounding communities in Ashanti Region for Electricity Company of Ghana (ECG). Outside the borders of her home country (Ghana), Ms. Dadzie, for two years managed the integration of the Somali and Bosnian people groups in a typical US workplace in Columbus, Ohio; handling sensitive cultural, social and religious norms, beliefs and practices of each group vis-à-vis that of the typical US societal and corporate expectations, and Ohio Labour Laws and Regulations. She is also an astute Management Strategist with several years’ experience in the management of multi-geographical location project teams in the Aviation Information Technology industry and rose to become the Country Manager for Amadeus Nigeria, in charge of Ghana and Nigeria, communicating and managing the expectations of various project proponents. Additional training and skills Linka has acquired include Social-Psychology of Minority Groups, Report Writing, Proofreading, Editing, and Project Management. She is a member of the International Association of Impact Assessors (IAIA). Linka is the Head of Communication and Social Development Unit at NEMAS Consult.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
