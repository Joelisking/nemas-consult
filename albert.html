MR. ALBERT ATISIKA

Deputy Environmental Quality and Surveillance Analyst


Mr. Albert Atisika holds a BSc in Environmental Management from the Presbyterian University College, Ghana. He has additional training in Computer Programming and Website Development and manages the IT related issues of his company in addition to his core functions.
Mr. Atisika, since joining NEMAS, has been involved in environmental quality monitoring and data analysis both in greenfield and brownfield projects. He played key roles in various projects, including; IronRidge Lithium Resources Limited (Now Atlantic Lithium Limited) Baseline Studies; Environmental and Impact Assessment for Kingsperp Mining Limited. He also takes active part in carrying out environmental analysis for industries such as Yara Ghana Limited, Heidelberg Cement Group (Ghacem Cement), Ghana Oil Palm Development Company (GOPDC), Azar Chemical Company Limited, Cosmo Seafood and Kasapreko Company, to enable these companies meet regulatory requirements. Albert is very meticulous both on the field and at the office. He takes active part in all environmental monitoring programs as part of EIA, PER, EMP and for industrial requirement for Environmental Performance Ratings (Akoben) by the EPA of Ghana. Albert works at the Environmental Quality and Surveillance Unit of NEMAS Consult.

About Us

NEMAS CONSULT LIMITED is an Environmental, Social and Safety Impacts and Risks Management consulting firm with its head office in Accra, Ghana. We offer end-to-end EHSS solutions that best support our clients' business needs and suit their particular situations, including regulatory requirements, environmental and social due diligence, environmental permitting, environmental compliance, environmental management, occupational health and safety, natural resources management, and environmental laboratory analysis services.
